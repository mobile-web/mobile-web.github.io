Features

Create your own lessons

MWEB offers lesson manipulation and maintenance, the admin will be responsible for doing this, they can easily manipulate through the Administrator's page. They can also format the lessons through HTML format. Through this app, students can also view the lesson via offline.

Upload your own videos

MWEB also provides videos which contain lessons. The administrator will be responsible for doing this. In uploading a video there's various file format which is ".mp4" and the size of the video is not exceeded in 10mb.

Collaborate to other people

MWEB offers a forum that they can easily communicate to other people in the campus. They can easily post a topic in their mobile app and also like the post if they want it and they can also comment and the students will able to discuss about a specific lesson.

Group your section

In MWEB, Teachers can make a group in order to filter their students by section. They can easily organize their students by grouping and easy to remember the teacher and easily deliver all the activities to their respective students and controlling the number of students in the teacher module

Student management

MWEB offers a student management, which gives an authority to accessed the application. Administrators can easily manipulate the students using the back end.

Realtime Announcements

MWEB offers posting announcement in the backend the teacher will reponsible for doing this activity. The students will realtime notify if the teacher will post announcement and that alerts you if there are updates occurred on your account.

Downloadable contents

MWEB offers a downloadable contents. The teacher will capable for uploading a pdf file in their classroom. The students will able to see all the uploaded pdf in their classroom and they easily download it.

Create your own quizzes

MWEB offers a manipulating a quiz in the back end and the teacher will be responsible for doing this. There are 3 kinds of quiz that MWEB offers first the multiple choice, second the true or false and the third is the fill in the blanks. The students will able to take the quiz via offline and online.

Drills

MWEB offers a drill module that will able to challenge their students. The purpose of this module is for the students to be able to send their work that was made in the proposed system to be for the teacher to check their work.

Rankings

MWEB offers a ranking features that will able to rank who will be get the highest score in quiz and in the drills


Mobile Web Compiler

MWEB Compiler

One of the main features of the Mweb is the compiler. That will able to simulate a php codes via mobile app, they can use this through offline and online. They can also change the color theme of the text editor and it offers a syntax highlighting.

This aims to test the psychomotor skills of the students by providing hands-on exercises through the use of a compiler. In the compiler module students can test their selves from what they learn to develop their skills in programming.
